Responsible AI Cannot Be an Afterthought, Needs to Be Part of DNA: Infosys CTO Rafee Tarafdar

👤
Editorial Board Interview Updated: May 25, 2026
🔗 Copy Link
Infosys CTO Mohammed Rafee Tarafdar advocates for proactive AI governance.

Nagpur, India — Mohammed Rafee Tarafdar is the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) of Infosys, responsible for steering the technology vision and engineering roadmap at the global IT giant. He founded the Strategic Technology Group, leading a elite workforce of technology advisors, full-stack architects, and elite programmers executing complex cloud and AI programs for global clients.

A distinguished Mechanical Engineering graduate from Karnatak University, Rafee is highly passionate about driving sustainable social impact and personally directs the Tech for Good program at the enterprise level.

"Tech for Good is a strategic area of focus. Given the scale of technologies we execute globally and in India, we can utilize them to drive massive social impact."

Speaking in an exclusive exchange, Tarafdar highlighted how Tech for Good fits into the organization’s ESG goals, emphasizing the critical importance of Responsible AI and its impact on the everyday common citizen.

Under the Tech for Good roadmap for 2025, Infosys successfully surpassed its goal to impact 80 million lives. The company is now actively outlining its strategic targets for 2030 to address key focus areas, including e-governance, accessible healthcare, and open education initiatives.
